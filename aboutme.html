About Me

My Journey

My passion for technology began at a young age when I first discovered the power of programming. What started as curiosity quickly evolved into a deep fascination with how computers can solve complex real-world problems. This journey led me to pursue Computer Science, where I found my calling at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity.

Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science at the University at Albany, where I'm diving deep into advanced topics like neural networks, cryptography, and distributed systems. My academic research focuses on developing AI-driven security solutions that can detect and prevent cyber threats in real-time.

Leadership & Extracurricular Experience

Beyond academics, I believe in the importance of leadership, teamwork, and community engagement. These experiences have shaped my ability to communicate effectively, manage projects, and inspire others.

Organizing Committee

I served as the Public Relations Lead for our college fest VIBHA, which saw a crowd of over 5,000 attendees. I took charge of inviting the band and DJ and coordinating celebrity invitations, ensuring smooth communication and event execution. As one of the main leads of the fest, I contributed to building partnerships, managing guest relations, and making the event a grand success.

Basketball Team Captain

As a passionate sportsperson, I had the privilege of leading Team VBIT as the Basketball Captain, guiding the team to win gold and bronze medals at the state level. Beyond basketball, I also represented Andhra Pradesh and Telangana at the national level in volleyball, earning a silver medal. These experiences strengthened my leadership, teamwork, and competitive spirit across multiple sports disciplines.

NGO

I worked as a Logistics Coordinator and Volunteer for the Blood Donation Camp organized by NSS, one of the largest student organizations. I was responsible for managing event logistics, coordinating with donors and medical teams, and ensuring the smooth execution of the camp. This experience strengthened my organizational, communication, and teamwork skills while contributing to a meaningful social cause.

Writer of the Month

I was honored to be named Writer of the Month at VBIT, where I secured first place in a poetry competition. This recognition highlighted my creativity and passion for writing, allowing me to express ideas and emotions through impactful words and storytelling.
