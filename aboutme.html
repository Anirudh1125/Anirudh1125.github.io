About Me

My Journey

My passion for technology began at a young age when I first discovered the power of programming. What started as curiosity quickly evolved into a deep fascination with how computers can solve complex real-world problems. This journey led me to pursue Computer Science, where I found my calling at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity.

Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science at the University at Albany, where I'm diving deep into advanced topics like neural networks, cryptography, and distributed systems. My academic research focuses on developing AI-driven security solutions that can detect and prevent cyber threats in real-time.
